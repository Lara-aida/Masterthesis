Out of the raw data, we can at least analyze the socio demographics of each participant. Each demographic information is grouped to evaluate its percentage in order to gain information about if the sample depicts the group of potential end users.

socio_demographics <- socio_demographics %>%
  mutate(
    sex = factor(sex, 
                 labels = c("Female", "Male")),
